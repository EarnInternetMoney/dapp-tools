DappHub is a community of developers, computer scientists, security researchers & category theorists working on blockchain software. Our suite of tools has grown through our work on Maker and other consulting projects and is designed to ease the correct construction and deployment of distributed applications. Our open source toolkit includes:

Dapp
build, test, deploy and transact with Ethereum contracts from the comfort of the command line.
Seth
slice and dice transactons, query the blockchain, convert between data formats, peform remote calls, estimate gas usage and more...
Hevm
an EVM implementation designed for testing and verification, with a nimble debugger that runs in the terminal.

We like tools that are fast, transparent, and good citizens of the command line, which is how we've built the dapp suite.
